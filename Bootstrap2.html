How to Make a Shrimp Ceviche Tray

Home> Recipes

Ceviche is a dish popular throughout Latin America that’s made with raw seafood “cooked” in citrus juices. I love it served as an appetizer or for a light meal. Shrimp ceviche is a happy food. Fresh, bright flavors and light shrimp make this one of my favorite warm weather dishes. It’s very easy to make, too. Please follow my recipe: three steps

Ingredients


  • Peeled Shrimp
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Chile Serrano
  • Black Pepper and Salt
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Purple Onion

Preparing

  1. Clean the Vegetables
  2. Cut the vegetables and set aside
  3. Marinate the shrimp with lime, Chile Serrano, Pepper & Salt and Cucumber

Marinating times make a difference when you are making ceviche. You will need at least 60 minutes for the citrus to “cook” the shrimp.
