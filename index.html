Pretty Women

Pretty Woman is a 1990 American romantic comedy film. The film's story centers on down-on-her-luck Hollywood prostitute Vivian Ward and wealthy businessman Edward Lewis. Vivian is hired to be Edward's escort for several business and social functions, and their relationship develops over the course of her week-long stay with him. The film saw the highest number of ticket sales in the US ever for a romantic comedy.
